Pasta Alla Norma

Ingredients:

  • 500g of pasta (penne, spaghetti or rigatoni)
  • 3 eggplants
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 8 San Marzano tomatoes
  • 60 grams of basil
  • 150 grams of salted ricotta to grate
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • pinch of salt
